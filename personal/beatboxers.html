Best Beatboxers by Category

Beatboxing has many categories, with many people that specialize in them. There have been categories that have been there since the beginning like solo and loopstation and there are categories that have been recently added such as crew and producer. Solo category is self explanatory, it's solo beatboxing. Loopstation is when someone loops sounds to make a track, possibly using a second device as well. Producer is the same but with the ability to use more devices. Tag team and crew are similar; tag team has two people beatboxing at once and crew has 3-5 people beatboxing at once.
