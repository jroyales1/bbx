Sounds by Difficulty

Beatboxing has a ton of creativity and potential when it comes to making sounds, allowing the use and creation of many different sounds that could be made from the human mouth.

Basic Kick Make a "B" sound with your lips K Snare Place your tongue on the roof of your mouth and inhale Hi Hat Make a "Tsk" sound with your mouth but without the K sound
Easy Trumpet Put your top teeth on your bottom lip and hum Zipper Choose a side on your lips and put them together, then suck in air through the lips, creating a vibration Water Drop Click with your tongue and whistle with using the tongue, not breathing
Medium Inward Bass Inhale air through the throat to make a vibration in the throat (difficult and needs practice) Sub Bass Lip Roll Use the same position as the zipper but make the lips more loose, making a low frequency vibration when inhaling air Clap Snare Place a one lip over the other (could be any way) and try to blow a raspberry
Hard Vibration Bass Do the same as the inward bass, but use more power Polyphonics Sing in falsetto, then try to whisper with it, then imagine the sound going down your throat Poh Snare Make a "pf" sound with your mouth and add a whistle at the end
